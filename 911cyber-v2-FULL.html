Common questions

What you need to know

Honest answers. No marketing spin. We believe transparency builds trust.

Can you guarantee I'll get my account or money back?
No — and anyone who makes that guarantee is not being honest with you. Outcomes depend on timing, platform policies, the type of attack, and how quickly you act. What we guarantee is fast, professional, expert-guided response to give you the best possible chance of recovery.
How fast will someone respond to my request?
During active operating hours, we aim to respond within minutes — not hours. Hot leads (active incidents) are routed immediately to an incident specialist. If your situation is active right now, use the WhatsApp button for the fastest possible connection.
Is my information kept confidential?
Absolutely. All communications and case details are handled with strict confidentiality. We do not share, publish, or disclose your information to any third party. Your privacy is fundamental to how we operate — many of our clients are professionals, executives, and business owners who require complete discretion.
What if I'm not sure whether I've been scammed or hacked?
That's exactly why we start with an emergency assessment. If you suspect something is wrong — unusual account activity, suspicious messages sent from your account, funds you don't recognize moving — contact us immediately. It's always better to assess and find nothing than to wait and lose everything.
Do you engage in hacking or illegal activities to recover accounts?
Never. We operate entirely within legal and ethical boundaries. Our methods involve expert guidance on platform recovery processes, account security protocols, reporting to authorities, and documentation support. Anyone offering "illegal recovery" is running a secondary scam — and we are not that.
What types of scams and attacks do you handle?
We handle: social media account takeovers (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, TikTok), email compromise (personal and business), crypto and investment scams, romance scams, phishing attacks, business email fraud (BEC), impersonation attacks, ransomware incidents, and admin lockouts. If you're unsure — just reach out and describe what happened.
Can you help if the incident happened days or weeks ago?
Yes, though the options narrow with time. Older incidents are harder to resolve, but we can still help with documentation, platform appeals, legal reporting, and security hardening. Contact us regardless of when it happened — we'll be honest about what is still possible.
